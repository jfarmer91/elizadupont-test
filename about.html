Eliza grew up on a winding dirt road in the backwoods of Vermont with her two loving parents and little sister. Staying active, getting outside and traveling were always engrained as core principles of a happy life. Coming from an artistic family, Eliza found photography as her medium of choice.

Eliza studied Art and Psychology as an undergraduate at the University of Vermont with a focus in Photography and Social Psychology. Throughout that time, she pursued portrait photography using both film and digital. She now works independently as a freelance photographer based out of Burlington, VT.
